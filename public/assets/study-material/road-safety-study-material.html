Telangana Road Safety Month 2026

Comprehensive Guide for Event Organisers

1. Helmet Safety

Mandatory Rules:
  • Always wear a BIS-certified helmet while riding a two-wheeler
  • Secure the chin strap snugly
  • Ensure pillion riders also wear helmets
  • Replace damaged or old helmets immediately

2. Seatbelt Discipline

Essential Guidelines:
  • Fasten seatbelts in every seat of the vehicle
  • Child passengers must use age-appropriate safety seats
  • Never start driving without buckling up
  • Check seatbelt functionality regularly

3. Speed Limits

Speed Awareness:
  • Follow posted speed limits strictly
  • Reduce speed in residential areas (30-40 km/h)
  • School zones: Maximum 25 km/h
  • Highways: Follow designated speed limits
  • Always slow down at zebra crossings

4. Traffic Signals

Signal Compliance:
  • Red: Complete stop, no exceptions
  • Yellow: Prepare to stop, do not accelerate
  • Green: Proceed with caution
  • Never jump signals or make illegal turns

5. Pedestrian Safety

Pedestrian Priority:
  • Always stop for pedestrians at zebra crossings
  • Give way to pedestrians at intersections
  • Avoid distractions while driving near pedestrian areas
  • Be extra cautious near schools and hospitals

6. Prohibited Behaviors

Strictly Avoid:
  • Drunk driving or riding under influence
  • Using mobile phones while driving
  • Triple riding on two-wheelers
  • Wrong-side driving or riding
  • Overtaking in no-overtaking zones
  • Parking on footpaths or blocking zebra crossings

7. Emergency Procedures

In Case of Accidents:
  • Immediately call emergency services: 108
  • Provide first aid if trained
  • Do not move injured persons unless necessary
  • Document the scene with photos if safe
  • Cooperate with authorities

8. Best Practices for Organisers

Event Management:
  • Ensure all participants follow safety guidelines
  • Provide safety equipment (helmets, reflective vests)
  • Plan routes with safety in mind
  • Have first aid kits readily available
  • Coordinate with local traffic police
  • Maintain proper documentation

9. Reference IDs

Each organiser receives unique reference IDs for tracking and verification purposes. Keep these IDs safe and share them with participants as needed.
